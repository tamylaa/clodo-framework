# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful and considerate of different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to `product@clodo.dev` or open a confidential issue and add the label `security`.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers will review and respond to reports and may take action as needed to ensure a safe community.
